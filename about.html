Welcome to Wealth Craft Capital Ventures Loan Company

At Wealth Craft Ventures, we understand the importance of financial flexibility and the impact it can have on individuals and businesses. With years of experience in the financial industry, we strive to provide accessible and tailored loan solutions to meet the unique needs of our clients.

▪ Wealth Craft Capital Ventures Limited (“Wealth Craft”) was established on 26th June 2019 and started operations in April 2020. ▪ The non bank financial institution was established to address the short to medium term financial gaps that individuals and business owners face.

▪ Wealth Craft provides loans to individuals employed by the Banks and other private sectors and these loans are secured by their salary payments, and collateral based loans to SME’s. ▪ Wealth Craft is head quartered in Lusaka

Trust Wealth Craft Ventures Loan Company for reliable and customer-centric financial services. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients and supporting them on their journey towards financial success.
